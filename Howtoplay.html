🏸 Badminton

Fast, fun, and competitive!

What is it about?

Badminton is one of the fastest racket sports in the world. It requires quick reactions, coordination, and strong focus.

Cool Facts

  • The fastest badminton smash ever recorded is over 400 km/h.
  • Badminton became an Olympic sport in 1992.
  • The shuttlecock is made from feathers or plastic.
  • Top players train for hours every day.
  • A match can last over an hour at professional level.
  • China has won the most Olympic badminton medals.
  • Badminton is one of the most popular sports in Asia.
  • It burns a lot of calories and improves fitness quickly.

Why Play Badminton?

Badminton improves fitness, reflexes, and coordination. It’s also a great way to stay active and have fun with friends.
