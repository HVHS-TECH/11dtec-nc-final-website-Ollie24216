🏸 Badminton

Fast, fun, and competitive!

What is it about?

Badminton is one of the fastest racket sports in the world. It requires quick reactions, good coordination, and strong focus. Players must think fast and move even faster to keep up with the shuttlecock.

It may look easy at first, but once you play it properly, you realise how intense and skill-based it really is.

Cool Facts

  • The shuttlecock can travel over 300 km/h in professional games.
  • Badminton has been an Olympic sport since 1992.
  • It is especially popular in countries like China, Indonesia, and India.
  • Professional matches require insane stamina and speed.

Why Play Badminton?

Badminton is not just fun—it’s also really good for your health. It improves fitness, coordination, and reflexes. It also helps with concentration and decision-making.

Another great thing is that you don’t need a lot of equipment, so it’s easy to play with friends at school or at home.
